# Infected 🧬

A first-person horror survival game built in Godot 4 for the Godot Wild Jam (Theme: Mutation).

## 📖 Story

You were bitten by a mutant specimen during a containment breach at a research facility. The mutation spreads rapidly through your body, transforming you from the inside out. You have **30 minutes** to find 3 antidote doses hidden across the facility — or become the very thing you fear.

## 🎮 Gameplay

- **First-Person Exploration** - Navigate through a dangerous research facility
- **Survival Horror** - Explore while racing against your mutation
- **30-Minute Countdown Timer** - Tension increases as time runs out
- **3 Antidote Doses** - Locate and collect doses hidden across 3 rooms
- **Progressive Mutation** - Visual and mechanical changes as the infection spreads

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Godot 4.2** - Game engine
- **GDScript** - Game scripting language
- **Blender** - 3D asset creation

## 📋 Features

- Immersive first-person perspective
- Atmospheric horror environment
- Real-time mutation progression
- Challenging exploration puzzles
- Time-pressure gameplay mechanics
