# Infected 🧬

A first-person horror survival game built in Godot 4 
for the Godot Wild Jam (Theme: Mutation).

## Story
You were bitten by a mutant specimen during a containment 
breach at a research facility. The mutation spreads fast.
You have 30 minutes to find 3 antidote doses hidden across 
the facility — or become what you fear.

## Gameplay
- First-person exploration and survival
- 30 minute countdown timer
- 3 antidote doses to find across 3 rooms
- Mutation worsens as time runs out

## Built With
- Godot 4.2
- GDScript
- Blender (assets)
